### Lecture & Active Listening (LEARN)

Disposition of the Lecture-tab / active-listening domain against the actual codebase. The reading surface is `web/components/workspace/LectureTab.tsx`, mounted read-only-tab by `WorkspaceShell.tsx` at `/topics` (auth-gated via `AppShell`). It is a faithful, partially-interactive screen (claim selection + tab routing work) but its content is entirely hardcoded mock data (`const CLAIMS`), not read from the trust ledger. The Active-Listening config lives at `web/app/settings/active-listening/page.tsx` (static). The trust invariants exist and are unit-tested in the domain engine (`web/lib/domain/trust.ts`, `types.ts`) but are not wired to the lecture surface.
