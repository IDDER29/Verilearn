### Lecture & Active Listening (LEARN)

Disposition of the Lecture-tab / active-listening user stories against the current codebase. Primary surface is `web/components/workspace/LectureTab.tsx` (a `"use client"` component) fed by `web/lib/services/workspace.ts` → `getTopicView`. The domain engine (`web/lib/domain/trust.ts`, `types.ts`) is the source of the trust vocabulary. Core finding: the lecture reader shows **real ledger aggregates** (percent-verified, trust-state counts, claim/source counts, title, level from `WorkspaceData`), but the **inline prose, the underlined claims, the claim-detail side rail, the section strip, section progress, all five active-listening prompts, and the close gate are hardcoded Dijkstra demo content** — not derived from real ledger claim entries. There is no section model, no per-learner reading/gate-state persistence, and `GapOrigin` (`gap.ts:40`) has no `"lecture"` value, so no story that depends on section navigation, functional gates, gate persistence, or lecture-origin misconceptions is fully wired.
