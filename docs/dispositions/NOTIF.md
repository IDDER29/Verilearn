### Notifications, Reminders & Messaging (NOTIF)

The domain is implemented as a single read-only derived view (`web/lib/services/notifications.ts`, `listNotifications`) computed over existing state — verification-ready topics, FSRS due-count, and disputed claims — rendered at the `/notifications` route (`web/app/notifications/page.tsx`). There is **no persistent notification store, no emit/event pipeline, no reminder scheduler, no channel fan-out (email/push), no preference model, and no webhooks**. So the in-app center exists and is wired to real data, but everything past the in-app read surface (delivery, consent, batching, scheduling) is unbuilt. There are also **no tests** for this service (236-test suite covers other services; none reference notifications).
