### Notifications, Reminders & Messaging (NOTIF)

The domain is implemented as a single read-only derived view (`web/lib/services/notifications.ts`, `listNotifications`) computed over existing state — verification-ready topics, FSRS due-count, and disputed claims — rendered at the `/notifications` route (`web/app/notifications/page.tsx`). A real per-category and per-item preference model DOES exist (`UserPrefs.notifications`/`review.reminders`, NOTIF-08) and is enforced at feed time. There is still **no persistent notification store, no emit/event pipeline, no reminder scheduler, no channel fan-out (email/push), and no webhooks** — so the in-app center exists and is wired to real data and real preferences, but everything past the in-app read surface (delivery, batching, scheduling, channels) is unbuilt.
