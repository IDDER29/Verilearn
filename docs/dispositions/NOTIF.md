### Notifications, Reminders & Messaging (NOTIF)

There is **no notification backend** in the codebase: no canonical notification store, no emit/dedup pipeline, no reminder scheduler, no channel fan-out, and no preference/consent engine. The only artifacts are a faithful **static** `/notifications` feed (`web/app/notifications/page.tsx`: hardcoded "2 unread", Today/Earlier layout, `All · Tests · Verification · Community` chips as inert `<span>`s, non-wired "Mark all read") and two static Settings toggles (`web/app/settings/review/page.tsx` "Daily reminder", `web/app/settings/privacy/page.tsx` "Product emails"). Several upstream *event sources* the domain would render are implemented in the engine (pipeline, FSRS due-counts, tests, gap lifecycle, cert revoke), but nothing emits a notification from them. Nothing here is fully Done.
