### Accessibility, Mobile & Offline (A11Y)

This is a cross-cutting non-functional domain. The pure domain engine (server-authoritative clocks, idempotent scheduling, RBAC/epistemic firewall, ledger objects) provides sound foundations, but the accessibility, responsive-mobile, and offline/PWA *surfaces and infrastructure* this domain owns are almost entirely unbuilt: no service worker, no PWA manifest, no `prefers-reduced-motion`, no skip link/landmarks, no mobile bottom-tab shell, no accommodation flag, no VPAT. The wired app is desktop-only static/mock UI.
