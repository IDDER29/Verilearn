### Platform Admin, Moderation & T&S (ADMIN)

Honest, evidence-based disposition of the Platform Administration, Content Moderation & Trust & Safety stories against the current codebase. The master invariant of this domain — the administrative/epistemic firewall (no admin role can write a trust state, resolve a Conflict on the merits, promote a source, or mint a certificate) — is the one piece that is fully implemented and load-time-enforced in code (`lib/domain/rbac.ts`). Most of the operational surfaces (moderation queue, audit store, quarantine action, ban enforcement, status page) are not built; there is no admin/T&S UI (`app/` has no `admin` or `moderation` route). Sandbox and real-model machinery is Deferred behind the `DeterministicVerifier` seam.
