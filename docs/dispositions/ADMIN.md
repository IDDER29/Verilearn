### Platform Admin, Moderation & T&S (ADMIN)

Honest, evidence-based disposition of the Platform Administration, Content Moderation & Trust & Safety stories against the current codebase. The master invariant of this domain — the administrative/epistemic firewall (no admin role can write a trust state, resolve a Conflict on the merits, promote a source, or mint a certificate) — is the one piece that is fully implemented and load-time-enforced in code (`lib/domain/rbac.ts`). Four real, RBAC-gated T&S consoles now exist under `app/admin/`: certificate revoke/reinstate (ADMIN-15/22), account ban/unban (ADMIN-16), claim quarantine (ADMIN-14), and a central audit log spanning all three (ADMIN-20) — each backed by real domain logic, not static screens, and each unreachable by a role lacking the matching permission. The unbuilt surfaces left are the ones genuinely gated on infrastructure that doesn't exist in this environment (real execution sandbox, orchestration telemetry) or on an unbuilt Community/reputation data model (moderation queue, vote-voiding, reputation-anomaly detection). Sandbox and real-model machinery is Deferred behind the `DeterministicVerifier` seam.
