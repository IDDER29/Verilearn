### Tests, Certificates & Verification (TEST)

Honest, evidence-based disposition of the TEST domain (`docs/prd/28-tests-certs.md`) against the current codebase. The pure engines (`web/lib/domain/tests-engine.ts`, `web/lib/domain/certificates.ts`) and their tests are strong; the service layer (`web/lib/services/tests.ts`, `web/lib/services/testsession.ts`) wires eligibility and real fail-closed certificate issuance. But the timed MCQ **runner is static** (explicitly Deferred — the deterministic verifier produces no MCQ options), there is **no public verify route**, and misses/gaps/readiness/retake are not yet driven by the engines end-to-end.
