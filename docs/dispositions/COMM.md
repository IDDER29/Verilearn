### Community, Contributions & Reputation (COMM)

The Community domain is essentially unbuilt as a data/logic layer. Two static UI screens exist — `web/app/community/page.tsx` (thread list, hot-debate feature, top-contributors leaderboard, "Verified answers only" explainer, New-thread button) and `web/app/community/[slug]/page.tsx` (OP, Skeptic "AI · sourced" answer block, human replies with like counts, reply composer, linked-claim panel). Both render entirely hardcoded arrays; nothing is wired to real data, persisted, or interactive. There is **no** community entity model in `web/lib/store`, **no** community service in `web/lib/services`, and **no** community domain engine. The only genuine, tested backing is the RBAC matrix (`web/lib/domain/rbac.ts` + `rbac.test.ts`): roles `sme_reviewer` (with `source:promote`, `review:submit`), `community_mod` (with `community:moderate`), and the epistemic firewall — `trust:write` granted to **no** human role (`FIREWALL_PERMISSIONS`). That firewall is the one invariant this domain relies on that is actually implemented and enforced.
