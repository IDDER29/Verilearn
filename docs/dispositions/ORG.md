### Organization / Team Administration (ORG)

Honest disposition of the ORG domain against the actual codebase. The build invested deeply in the pure domain engine, RBAC, identity, and tenant-scoped persistence, but the B2B admin-console surface (roster, invites, cohorts, assignments, library governance, visibility policy, audit UI) is not yet constructed. Where a story's core invariant lives in the engine (the epistemic firewall, disputed-exclusion, separable least-privilege roles), that is cited as real, tested evidence; the user-facing provisioning workflows on top are not wired.
