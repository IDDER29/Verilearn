### Gap Map & Misconception Tracking (GAP)

The pure lifecycle engine (`web/lib/domain/gap.ts`, 22 tests in `gap.test.ts`) is complete and honest: `open → watching → closed → reopened`, evidence-gated close (`closeGap` throws `GapEvidenceError` below `MASTERY_THRESHOLD`), auto-reopen on lapse (`onLapse` / `applyReviewOutcome`), severity escalation on reopen (`bumpSeverity`), append-only history, and documented illegal-transition no-ops that never fabricate a false state. Gaps are persisted (`GapRecord` in `web/lib/store/entities.ts`, `db.gaps` + `gapsOf` in `web/lib/store/db.ts`, seeded `gap_1` in `web/lib/store/seed.ts`). The ONE place the engine is wired to live app data is review grading: `gradeCard` (`web/lib/services/review.ts:43-56`) auto-reopens (or opens) a gap on an `again` lapse and persists it. There is **no gap board, detail view, empty state, revisit action, drill-seed UI, discussion thread, group-by, or reset wiring** anywhere in `app/` — every UI story below is engine/data-only.
