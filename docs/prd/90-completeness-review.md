### 3. Prioritized remaining-risk backlog — the P0 pre-build workstream

*The critique lenses (`50–56`) **surface** four Blocker-grade risks that the domain specs do **not yet resolve**. These are not hygiene items and not "found later" items: each falsifies a headline claim of the product's own thesis, each already has a scoped decision in `02`'s Open Decisions, and each must be closed before committing spend **beyond** the proven core loop. Severity is **P0** for all four by construction — they gate "complete" sign-off, not just a downstream tier.*

| # | P0 workstream item | Owner-type | Why it is P0 (which thesis claim it falsifies if unaddressed) | Register anchor |
|---|--------------------|-----------|--------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------|
| **P0-1** | **Credential independence / the self-signed certificate.** For Free/Pro self-directed learners (the majority of all cert-earners) there is no SME: the learner authors the topic, adjudicates their own Skeptic conflicts, attaches their own sources, passes an unproctored open-book test drawn from those claims, and names the credential — while the public verify page attests "verified & sourced" without disclosing the absence of independent review (`52 §1,7`; `53 §1`). | **Product / Research** (credential strategy) | The certificate is the product's flagship, monetizable, employer-facing output; the moment one employer or journalist notices they can mint themselves an "Advanced X, ≥75%, verified-only" cert on a topic they wrote and graded, the credential's market value **and** the honesty brand collapse in a single tweet. Decide the independence ladder (self-study → peer → SME → proctored) and disclose it. | ASM-17, ASM-20, ASM-24 → **OD-1** (+ OD-7) |
| **P0-2** | **Automated-verifier evaluation harness with measured precision *and* recall.** Skeptic accuracy is measured only as "SME agreement on flagged items" — precision on disputes it *did* raise. **Recall is invisible by construction:** a confidently-wrong claim shipped as `Verified` and never disputed leaves no trace, and model rollouts have no accuracy gate (`53 §2,3`; `50 §8`). | **AI-Evaluation / Research** (Model-Quality owner) | The verifier is the single instrument the entire product rests on; "an honesty-branded product cannot let its own verifier's accuracy be an article of faith." Endorsed error — a wrong claim wearing a green badge — is the one failure the product exists to prevent, and today it is uncountable. Stand up a versioned per-domain gold set as a hard rollout gate + canary, plus a continuous SME audit sample of *already-Verified* claims to make recall observable; publish both figures. | ASM-01, ASM-02, ASM-03, ASM-07 → **OD-4** (+ OD-6) |
| **P0-3** | **AI-cost governor for unit economics.** Pro "unlimited" runs a 6-stage LLM pipeline + Skeptic + execution sandbox (+ hard mode + effectively-unlimited revise-loop grading) with **no fair-use ceiling, no verification-credit metering, and no per-account budget**; the target personas (LEARNER-POWER, LEARNER-SKEPTIC) are structurally guaranteed-loss accounts, and per-user free-text topics amortize into **no shared verified-corpus asset** (`55`; `53 §10`; `56`). | **Product / Finance** | The business model "loses more money the better it does" — gross margin is uncapped-negative on exactly the users the product is built for, and the moat never compounds because every topic is paid in full, forever. Meter the expensive levers (hard mode, thorough re-verification) under a generous credit pool + overage, and pair it with a content-addressable canonical verified-ledger cache — the only path from O(users×topics) toward O(unique claims). | ASM-15, ASM-16, ASM-27, ASM-28 → **OD-2** |
| **P0-4** | **SME supply / queue capacity model.** The human SME sits on the critical path of *every* trust guarantee (publish/assign gate, source promotion, conflict resolution, quarantine release, injection containment, post-rollout re-review, cert-fraud review) yet is **unmodeled** for who they are, how they're sourced/paid/scaled, their per-hour throughput, triage priority, backlog SLA, and overflow behavior (`53 §9`; `55`; `56`). | **Product / Ops** | The availability of the honesty promise is bounded by human-review rate, and that bound is unmodeled — the realistic scale failure is "correct but unavailable." A backlogged SME queue silently blocks Teams publishing and cert issuance and shrinks the certifiable scope; unpriced staff SMEs destroy margin. Model the queue as an SLO'd, tenant-fair, prioritized subsystem with batch tooling **before** the Teams/B2B spend that exposes it. | ASM-18, ASM-19, ASM-20 → **OD-3** |

**One closely-linked fifth blocker, same owner.** Measurement-validity of the four honest signals — "Transfer" measured on the taught claim set (near-retention wearing a transfer label) and "Calibration" computed against a self-reported recall proxy (`50 §1–3`; ASM-10/11/12) — is the natural fifth P0, owned by the same Research/psychometric function as P0-2 and resolvable in part for free (rename "Transfer" → "Application" per **OD-8**). It rides alongside the four above; an honesty brand must not ship signals its own Skeptic would flag.

*The `50–56` lenses also carry a deep P1/P2 tail — indirect prompt injection via retrieved/promoted content (`53 §4`), quote-level grounding for `Verified·source` (`53 §5`), test-taker identity/proctoring and item security (`52 §2,3`), the ~8 unowned integrity/quality roles (G10), the engagement/habit half (`51`), data-residency/DPA/cookie-consent instruments (`54`), the ledger-change event bus and canonical store (`56`), and the guardian-oversight model (`54 §2`). These sequence into the relevant tier (certificates / Teams) and the build phase; they are not gating "complete" sign-off the way P0-1…4 are.*

### 4. Readiness verdict

**Verdict: The PRD is now build-ready to found engineering of the MVP core loop and its legal/security floor with eyes open, and — with the assumptions register (`02`) present and the exec summary (`01`) refreshed — the two hygiene gaps that blocked the prior pass are closed. What remains before a full "complete" sign-off is no longer artifacts or contradictions; it is the four-item P0 pre-build workstream (credential independence, verifier eval harness, cost governor, SME supply/queue model), which the lenses diagnose as Blockers and `02`'s Open Decisions already scope into concrete decisions and experiments.**

What is genuinely done and strong: the single-learner verification loop is specified to an unusually high standard (462 stories, each with acceptance criteria, invariants, cross-domain contracts, and failure/edge handling), the epistemic-firewall invariant reads consistently across all 20 domains, and the connective scaffolding is now complete and sound — **`45` traceability** proves every one of the 24 personas' goals lands on a real surface with a real technical obligation and declares (with a candid orphan list) that no surface is unanchored; **`40` prioritization** gives a defensible MVP decision rule, a reconciled 203/222/27/10 MoSCoW rollup, and an R1/R2/R3 sequence that correctly pulls COPPA age-gating, the encryption baseline, sub-processor DPAs, and the breach workflow **into R1** via real SEC-* stories; **`02` assumptions** states the 28 load-bearing premises and 8 open decisions the earlier pass found missing; and **`39` security + `50–56` lenses** close the audit-coverage holes so no major area is un-examined. A competent team can start the MVP loop from `20–39` today, sequenced by `40`, with `45` as the coverage spine and `02` as the risk ledger.

What still blocks calling it *complete*: the deepest substantive risks are **documented but unresolved** — a self-signed credential for the majority of learners (`52`/`53`), an unvalidated verifier with unmeasurable recall (`53`), unit economics that lose more money the better the product does (`55`), and an SME bottleneck with no supply or capacity model (`53`/`56`). These are Blocker-tagged analyses with scoped experiments, not closed items, and the domain specs have not yet been revised to incorporate their fixes or to add the ~8 unowned integrity/quality roles.

**Honest bottom line.** The thinness is no longer "whole areas are un-audited" and no longer "the connective tissue is missing" — both are fixed. It is that **the hardest, most existential questions the product's own honesty standard demands — does the certificate mean anything for a self-verified topic? is the verifier itself accurate? does the money and the human-SME throughput close? — are surfaced as owned, scoped, open blockers rather than answered.** Recommendation: **do not yet mark the PRD "complete."** Treat P0-1…4 (with signal-validity as the linked fifth) as a required pre-build workstream, executed against `02`'s Open Decisions, before committing spend beyond the core loop. The differentiator is real and now well-specified end to end, front to back; the credential-integrity, AI-reliability, and business/scale envelope around it is diagnosed, owned, and de-risking-planned — but not yet closed.
