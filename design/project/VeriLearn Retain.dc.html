Area 4 · Retain — review session

One session, a loop of states

Spaced repetition with a conscience. Every card is confidence-gated before reveal, misconceptions are caught separately from simple forgetting, and seeded error-drills test whether the learner can spot a wrong claim. The states below are the steps of a single session.

R1Card + gateRate confidence before revealing.
R2Reveal + rateSee answer + source, grade recall.
R3Wrong ideaMisconception path, not just "forgot".
R4Seeded drillSpot the deliberately-wrong claim.
R5CompleteSummary + blind-spot score.

The loop: R1 → R2 → next card, repeating through the queue. R3 branches off a "wrong idea" or an Again rating; R4 is a drill card mixed into the queue; R5 ends the session.

Reference

Rules of the session

Confidence gate
Reveal locked until Sure / Unsure / Guessing is chosen (when the gate is on).
Again rating
Surfaces the wrong-idea path and reschedules the card soon.
Seeded drill
Caught or missed updates the blind-spot score → drill-detection signal.
Gap map
Misconceptions can be promoted to tracked gaps that resurface.
Queue
Cards + drills progress through "Up next" until the session completes.
